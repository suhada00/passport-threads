Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

At Passport Threads, we prioritize the privacy and safety of our visitors. This Privacy Policy documents the types of information we collect and process when you use our website (passport-threads.com) and how we protect it.

1. Information We Process

We do not require any registration, sign-in, or connection to your Meta or Threads accounts. To generate your satirical passport, we only collect:

  • Username: The public Threads username you enter into the input form.
  • Manual Description: If your account is private and you decide to use "Manual Mode," we process the short self-description you write.
  • IP Address & Country: Used temporarily for rate-limiting requests and resolving your country for passport cover personalization.

2. No Permanent Data Storage

Your Threads profile data, avatar image, bio, and recent posts are fetched dynamically in the Cloudflare Worker server-side, analyzed to generate the satirical details, and immediately returned to your browser. We do not store your profile information or generated passport details on any database.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our site. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to other websites, specifically social media platforms (X/Twitter, Instagram, Threads, WhatsApp) and our blog. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site, which is governed by its own privacy terms.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@passport-threads.com.
