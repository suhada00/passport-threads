Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on Passport Threads (threadspassport.fun), and your choices regarding cookies settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following functions:

Preferences: We use cookies or localStorage to save your selected interface language (e.g. French, Spanish, Indonesian) so that the website remembers it on your next visit.

Advertising: We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our site. Google uses DART cookies to show ads matching your browsing history. We do not place cookies or track your browsing activity ourselves.

3. Managing Your Cookies Choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. However, doing so may affect some site features, such as language preference persistence. To opt-out of Google's personalized advertising cookies, you can visit Google's Ad Settings page.
